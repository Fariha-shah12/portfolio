Who I am

Data, decoded — then delivered.

Hi, I'm Fariha. I'm an MS Data Science graduate from Seattle University with a background in data engineering — I spent over four years building and maintaining production data systems before going back for my Master's to deepen my machine learning and analytics skillset.

I like the part of the job most people skip past: figuring out exactly what a question is really asking before writing a single line of SQL or Python. I thrive on building scalable data pipelines, crafting visualizations that actually explain something, and applying machine learning to turn raw, messy data into decisions people can act on.

Outside of data, I'm usually cycling somewhere scenic, chasing a good photo, trying a new cuisine, or working through a Sudoku. I've also gotten into Stoic philosophy lately — the practical, no-nonsense kind of wisdom fits how I like to approach problems, too.
